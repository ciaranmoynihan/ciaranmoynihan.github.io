CLOUD COMPUTING



Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)

Infrastructure as a Service is the most basic form that allows the user the most control over their own existing resources. Providers give access to servers and storage space.

Platform as a Service (PaaS)

Platform as a Service is full management of the service by a provider. The user needs only to manage their own files, and services, with distribution and storage managed by the provider.

Software as a Service (SaaS)

Software as a Service is the form that most people are aware of. This form is mostly seen as end-user applications, such as streaming services or as other subscription internet services.
